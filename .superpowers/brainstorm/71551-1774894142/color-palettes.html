Color Palette Direction

For an organic, living design with serif headings. Which palette feels like MentiSphere?

Deep Forest
Dark greens + warm parchment + gold accent
Knowledge grows in the dark
Earthy, deep, scholarly. Like an old library in a forest.

A — Deep Forest

Dark, lush greens with warm ivory backgrounds and gold accents. Feels ancient, grounded, and wise. High contrast between light and dark modes.

Coastal Dawn
Deep ocean + teal + warm terracotta
Where depth meets the horizon
Calm and expansive. Intelligence with warmth.

B — Coastal Dawn

Deep navy-teal gradient with warm stone backgrounds and terracotta highlights. Calm, expansive, and sophisticated. Balances depth with warmth.

Mycelium
Deep violet + lavender + warm copper
The network beneath the surface
Mysterious, interconnected, alive. Like neural pathways.

C — Mycelium

Deep violets and purples with warm copper accents on cream. Evokes neural networks and underground connections. Distinctive and memorable — no one else uses this palette for AI.

Amber Canopy
Deep brown + amber + sage green
Sunlight through ancient trees
Warm, natural, grounded. Knowledge as a living canopy.

D — Amber Canopy

Rich ambers and browns with sage green accents on warm ivory. Feels natural, grounded, and inviting. The warmest palette — like sunlit parchment and wood.
